# The Orientation Room — Brand Notes

## Voice
- Quiet, precise, warm and serious.
- Speaks like a trusted cartographer, not a guru, therapist, coach or SaaS brand.
- Confident enough to sell the room clearly: clarity, orientation, field maps, human attention and the next outer step.
- Uses direct second person language without hype, pressure or performance culture.

## Forbidden words
- hustle
- unlock your potential
- transformation package
- scale yourself
- guru
- hack
- mastermind
- crush it
- AI coach
- therapy replacement
- diagnosis

## Preferred words
- orientation
- clarity
- field
- map
- cartography
- threshold
- transition
- attention
- written field report
- next outer step
- active forces
- readable situation
- room

## Core promise
The Orientation Room helps a person in transition turn an unclear life or work situation into a readable field map and one concrete next outer step.

## Visual atmosphere
- A quiet cartography room: warm paper, dark ink, copper, moss and deep blue.
- Premium and atmospheric, but not glossy corporate.
- Serious and human, but not therapy beige.
- Visual cues: maps, grid lines, table light, field notes, contour lines, quiet archive textures, a real room or restrained portrait.
