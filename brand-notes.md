# The Orientation Room — Brand Notes

## Core promise
The Orientation Room turns a diffuse transition into a readable field map: active forces, central tension, possible paths, and one next outer step.

## Voice
- Quiet, precise, human, grounded.
- Speaks to intelligent people in transition without over-explaining them.
- Names pressure and complexity without dramatizing it.
- Sells the room as a guided cartography space, not as self-improvement performance.

## Preferred words
- Orientierung
- Feldkarte / Orientation Map
- Lage / Übergangslage
- Schwelle
- Kräfte
- zentrale Spannung
- nächster äußerer Schritt
- lesen, sortieren, verdichten, kartieren
- menschliche Aufmerksamkeit

## Forbidden or avoided words
- hustle
- optimize / Optimierung
- mindset hack
- breakthrough
- transformation promise
- guru language
- diagnosis language
- AI magic
- therapy replacement

## Visual atmosphere
- Warm paper, ink, copper, moss, deep blue.
- Cartographic, tactile, quiet, slightly archival.
- Spacious sections, slow rhythm, field notes rather than sales noise.
- Human trust before tool fascination.

## Boundary posture
Do not lead with disclaimers. Keep legal and care boundaries discreet near the bottom. State clearly that the work is orientation, psychoeducation, and structured reflection, and that crisis, diagnosis, medical care, and psychotherapy belong with licensed or emergency services.
