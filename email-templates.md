# Email Templates — The Orientation Room v0

## 1. Booking confirmation
Subject: Your Orientation Room is booked

Hi [Name],

thank you for booking The Orientation Room.

Time: [Date / Time]
Link / Location: [Link]

Before we meet, please fill out the short intake form:
[Intake Link]

Bring one concrete field of confusion: a transition, decision, pressure, or stuck place. We do not need to solve your whole life in one session. We need to make the current field readable.

Important: The Orientation Room is orientation work, not psychotherapy, diagnosis, medical treatment, or crisis care.

See you soon,

## 2. Preparation email
Subject: Before our Orientation Room

Hi [Name],

for tomorrow, please bring:

1. The current situation in plain words.
2. The strongest pressure or decision.
3. What feels old / dead / no longer carrying.
4. What might be new but has no form yet.

You do not need to be clear. That is the point of the room.

S

---

## 3. Delivery email
Subject: Your Orientation Map

Hi [Name],

attached is your Orientation Map.

It includes:
- current field
- active forces
- central tension
- old-system dependencies
- emerging possibilities
- blind spots / noise
- next external steps

Read it once slowly. Do not try to execute everything. Choose one next step and make it visible in the real world.

S
